  • Farmer Friends — The Earthworms
    Earthworms are one of the most important creatures in the world. They help to aerate and improve the quality of soil, which helps plants to grow better. They also help to decompose organic matter, which enriches the soil. Earthworms are a vital part of the ecosystem and play an important role in keeping it healthy. They also help to decompose organic matter, making it available for plants to use as nutrients. Earthworms are an important part of the food chain, providing food for many animals including birds, amphibians, reptiles, and mammals.
